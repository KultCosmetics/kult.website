Ordering and Payments

How do I place an order on your App?

You can place an order on our App by logging in, adding items to your cart, selecting a payment method, and completing the checkout process.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept a wide range of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, online banking, digital wallets, EMI, and Pay Later. You can choose the option that works best for you at the checkout page.

Are you currently offering Cash on Delivery (COD) as a payment option for orders?

As of now, we are not offering COD as a payment option for orders. However, we may consider offering COD as a payment option in the near future.

Is there a minimum order value required to place an order?

No, there is no minimum order value required to place an order on our App. You can order as much or as little as you need.

How can I be sure that my online purchases are secure?

We offer a guarantee of security for your payment and information by using SSL technology and partnering with certified e-payment vendors to ensure the safety of your online purchases.

What should I do if money is deducted but my order is not placed?

Shipping and Delivery

How does the Delivery Process Work?

  • Our system receive and process your order
  • Each products are inspected and pass through multiple round of quality checks
  • The products are packed safely and handed over to our trusted delivery partner
  • We try our best to deliver your order within the committed time.
  • In case our delivery partners are unable to contact you or unable to locate your provided address, you will be contacted from our end to resolve your concern.

How does my order get packed?

We pack your order in safe boxes. Each product is safely wrapped while fragile items are safely secured with an additional wrap layer.

‍

How much does shipping cost?

We're pleased to inform you that there are currently no shipping charges applicable to your order. For more details, please refer to our Terms of use policy.

‍

Do you offer international shipping?

Unfortunately, we do not provide international shipping at this time.

‍

What is the estimated delivery time for my order?

We strive to deliver orders in Mumbai within 1-2 days and in the rest of India within 3-5 days, excluding Sundays and public holidays. Please note that this is an estimated delivery time and may vary depending on the location and product availability and other circumstances. If you are ordering our products from a Mega Sale event, dispatches may be a bit delayed due to increased volumes.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Once your order has been shipped, a tracking number will be provided for you to track your package.

‍

Can I change my shipping address after my order has been placed?

If your order hasn't been shipped yet, we may be able to update the shipping address. Please contact our customer support team for assistance.

‍

What should I do if my shipping box / outer packaging is damaged?

We request you to not accept the package if it is damaged. In case of any such event, please contact customer support within 48 hours.

What if my package is lost or missing?

A: If your package is missing, we suggest checking with your neighbours or family members, and contacting the courier partner to locate the package. If the package is still not found, please reach out to our customer support for further assistance.
