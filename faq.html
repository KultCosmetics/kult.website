Shipping and Delivery

How does the Delivery Process Work?

  • Our system receives and processes your order
  • Each product is inspected and pass through multiple rounds of quality checks
  • The products are packed safely and handed over to our trusted delivery partner

  • In case our delivery partners are unable to contact you or unable to locate your address, you will be contacted from our end to resolve your concern.

We pack your order in safe boxes. Each product is safely wrapped while fragile items are safely secured with an additional wrap layer.
